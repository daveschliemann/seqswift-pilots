About the Founder

SeqSwift is built by Dave Schliemann, MD – a fourth-year international medical graduate completing clinical training in the United States.

During a recent family-medicine clerkship, the young daughter of his preceptor – a little girl he and the team cared for together in clinic – died of an overwhelming infection that rapid diagnostics might have caught sooner.

Those two losses made the mission personal: no child, anywhere, should die because a genomic result took days instead of seconds.

No big lab. No big budget.
Just code that refuses to let the next child wait.
